Antidiscrimination Notice

It is an unlawful employment practice for an employer to fail or refuse to hire or discharge any individual, or otherwise to discriminate against any individual with respect to that individual's terms and conditions of employment, because of such individual's race, color, religion, sex, or national origin. This employer is subject to certain governmental recordkeeping and reporting requirements for the administration of civil rights laws and regulations.

To comply with these laws, this employer invites employees to voluntarily self-identify their race/ethnicity and gender. If you do not self-identify your race/ethnicity at this time, the federal government requires this information to be determined by the employer through a visual survey and/or other available information.

Submission of this information is voluntary, and refusal to provide it will not subject you to any adverse treatment. The information will be kept confidential and may only be used in accordance with the provisions of applicable laws, executive orders, and regulations.
